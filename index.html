About

The Artist

A Voice from Ghana

Akuamoah is a Ghanaian singer, songwriter, and performer whose music blends the soulful rhythms of her homeland with contemporary elegance. Born and raised in the vibrant city of Accra, she crafts songs that carry emotion, culture, and story.

With every note, she invites listeners into a world where melody meets meaning — a world shaped by heritage, heart, and the timeless beauty of African sound.
